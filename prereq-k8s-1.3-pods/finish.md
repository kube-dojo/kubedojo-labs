## Lab Complete!

You've mastered the Pod lifecycle:

- **Imperative creation**: `kubectl run` — fast, great for one-off pods
- **Declarative creation**: Write YAML, `kubectl apply -f` — reproducible, version-controllable
- **Inspection**: `kubectl describe pod`, `kubectl logs`, `kubectl get pod -o wide` — understand what's happening
- **Deletion**: `kubectl delete pod` — clean up resources

In practice, you rarely create pods directly. Instead, you use Deployments (next lab) which manage pods for you. But understanding pods is essential — they're the foundation.
