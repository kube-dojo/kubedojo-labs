## Lab Complete!

You've mastered Kubernetes Services:

- **ClusterIP**: Internal-only access — the default service type, reachable only from within the cluster
- **NodePort**: External access — opens a port on every node, useful for development and testing
- **Connectivity**: `curl` from inside the cluster to verify services are routing traffic to pods

Services are the glue between your applications. Combined with Deployments, they form the foundation of every Kubernetes application.
