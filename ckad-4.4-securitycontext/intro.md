# SecurityContexts

In this lab you will configure pod and container-level security settings. SecurityContexts control user IDs, capabilities, and filesystem permissions.

**What you will learn:**
- runAsUser, runAsGroup, fsGroup
- Linux capabilities (add/drop)
- readOnlyRootFilesystem

**Time:** 30 minutes

**Curriculum module:** [CKAD Part 4 - SecurityContexts](https://kube-dojo.github.io/k8s/ckad/part4-environment/module-4.4-securitycontext/)
