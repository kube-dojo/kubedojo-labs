# Congratulations!

You have completed the Storage Management lab.

## What You Learned

- Listing block devices with `lsblk` and `blkid`
- Creating and mounting filesystems (tmpfs)
- Analyzing disk usage with `du` and `df`

## Key Takeaways

1. **lsblk** gives the best overview of storage topology
2. **tmpfs** is perfect for temporary high-speed storage
3. **du -sh | sort -rh** quickly finds space hogs
4. **Monitor both space and inodes** — you can run out of either
