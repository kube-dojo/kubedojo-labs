# Storage Management

Storage management is a fundamental sysadmin skill. This lab covers block device inspection, filesystem operations, and disk usage analysis.

## What You Will Learn

- Listing and inspecting block devices with `lsblk` and `blkid`
- Creating and mounting filesystems
- Analyzing disk usage with `du` and `df`

## Prerequisites

- Basic understanding of filesystems and mount points
- Root access (provided in this lab)
