# Exam Strategy

The CKA exam is 2 hours with 15-20 performance-based tasks. Time management is critical. You are allowed to access the official Kubernetes documentation during the exam.

In this lab you will:

- Prepare a bookmark list of essential documentation pages
- Practice prioritizing tasks by points-per-minute efficiency

**What you will learn:**
- Which documentation pages to bookmark before the exam
- The three-pass strategy for maximum points
- How to calculate task priority based on points and estimated time

**Time:** 15 minutes

**Curriculum module:** [CKA Part 0 - Exam Strategy](https://kube-dojo.github.io/k8s/cka/part0-environment/module-0.5-exam-strategy/)
