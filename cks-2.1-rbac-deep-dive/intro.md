# RBAC Deep Dive

RBAC is the primary authorization mechanism in Kubernetes and a core CKS domain (15% weight). This lab covers advanced RBAC patterns for security.

## What You Will Learn

- Design least-privilege Roles and ClusterRoles
- Bind roles correctly to users, groups, and ServiceAccounts
- Audit existing RBAC for over-permissioned accounts
- Prevent privilege escalation via RBAC misconfigurations
