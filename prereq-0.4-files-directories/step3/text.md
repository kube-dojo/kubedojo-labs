## Step 3: Permissions

Permissions control who can read, write, and execute files.

### Your task
1. Change the permissions of `~/lab-workspace/important_script.sh` to make it **executable** for the owner.
2. Change the permissions of `~/lab-workspace/secret_config.cfg` so it is **readable** by the owner.

<details>
<summary>Hint</summary>

```bash
chmod +x ~/lab-workspace/important_script.sh
chmod 600 ~/lab-workspace/secret_config.cfg
```
</details>
