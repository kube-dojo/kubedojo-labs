# CKS Exam Strategy

The CKS exam is 2 hours with approximately 15-20 tasks. The three-pass strategy helps maximize your score.

## Three-Pass Strategy

1. **Pass 1 (Quick wins)**: Tasks solvable in 1-3 minutes — security contexts, simple RBAC
2. **Pass 2 (Medium)**: Tasks taking 4-6 minutes — network policies, PSA configuration
3. **Pass 3 (Complex)**: Remaining time — audit logging, admission webhooks

## What You Will Practice

- Rapid security context fixes
- RBAC and NetworkPolicy creation
- Audit logging configuration
