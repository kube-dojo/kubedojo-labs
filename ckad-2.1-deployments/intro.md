# Deployments Deep Dive

In this lab you will master Kubernetes Deployments — the most common workload resource. Deployments manage ReplicaSets and enable rolling updates and rollbacks.

**What you will learn:**
- Create and scale deployments
- Perform rolling updates
- Roll back to previous revisions
- Debug stuck rollouts

**Time:** 35 minutes

**Curriculum module:** [CKAD Part 2 - Deployments](https://kube-dojo.github.io/k8s/ckad/part2-deployment/module-2.1-deployments/)
