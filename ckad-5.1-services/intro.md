# Services

In this lab you will create and test different Service types. Services provide stable networking endpoints for pods.

**What you will learn:**
- ClusterIP Services for internal communication
- NodePort Services for external access
- Headless Services and DNS resolution

**Time:** 30 minutes

**Curriculum module:** [CKAD Part 5 - Services](https://kube-dojo.github.io/k8s/ckad/part5-networking/module-5.1-services/)
