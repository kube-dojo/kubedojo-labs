# Volumes for Developers

In this lab you will work with Kubernetes volumes to persist data across container restarts. Understanding volumes is crucial for CKAD — you will encounter PVC creation and mounting frequently.

**What you will learn:**
- emptyDir for temporary shared storage
- hostPath for node-level storage
- PersistentVolume and PersistentVolumeClaim lifecycle

**Time:** 30 minutes

**Curriculum module:** [CKAD Part 1 - Volumes](https://kube-dojo.github.io/k8s/ckad/part1-design-build/module-1.4-volumes/)
