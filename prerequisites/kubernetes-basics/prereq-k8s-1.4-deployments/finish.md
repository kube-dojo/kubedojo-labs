## Lab Complete!

You've mastered the Deployment lifecycle:

- **Create**: `kubectl create deployment` — define your application
- **Scale**: `kubectl scale` — adjust the number of replicas
- **Update**: `kubectl set image` — rolling update with zero downtime
- **Rollback**: `kubectl rollout undo` — revert to a previous version instantly

Deployments are the workhorse of Kubernetes. They manage ReplicaSets, which manage Pods — a three-layer hierarchy that gives you declarative updates, self-healing, and easy rollbacks.
