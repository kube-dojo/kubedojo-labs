# CKAD Overview & Strategy

In this lab you will set up your exam environment, practice kubectl shortcuts, and complete a timed resource-creation challenge.

The CKAD exam is 2 hours with 15-20 performance-based tasks. Speed matters — every shortcut you master saves time for harder questions.

**What you will learn:**
- How to configure kubectl aliases and autocompletion
- Imperative command patterns that save minutes per task
- Time management with the three-pass strategy

**Time:** 25 minutes

**Curriculum module:** [CKAD Part 0 - CKAD Overview & Strategy](https://kube-dojo.github.io/k8s/ckad/part0-environment/module-0.1-ckad-overview/)
