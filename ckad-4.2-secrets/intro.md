# Secrets

In this lab you will create and use Kubernetes Secrets to manage sensitive data like passwords, tokens, and certificates.

**What you will learn:**
- Create generic, Opaque, and docker-registry Secrets
- Inject Secrets as environment variables
- Mount Secrets as files

**Time:** 30 minutes

**Curriculum module:** [CKAD Part 4 - Secrets](https://kube-dojo.github.io/k8s/ckad/part4-environment/module-4.2-secrets/)
