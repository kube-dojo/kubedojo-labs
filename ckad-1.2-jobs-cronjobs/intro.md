# Jobs and CronJobs

In this lab you will create Jobs for one-off batch processing and CronJobs for scheduled tasks. These are common CKAD exam topics.

**What you will learn:**
- Create Jobs with completions and parallelism
- Create CronJobs with cron schedules
- Handle Job failures and backoff limits
- Troubleshoot stuck Jobs

**Time:** 30 minutes

**Curriculum module:** [CKAD Part 1 - Jobs and CronJobs](https://kube-dojo.github.io/k8s/ckad/part1-design-build/module-1.2-jobs-cronjobs/)
