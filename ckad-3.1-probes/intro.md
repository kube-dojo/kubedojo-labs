# Application Probes

In this lab you will configure health probes — liveness, readiness, and startup — which tell Kubernetes when to restart containers, when to route traffic, and when an app has finished starting.

**What you will learn:**
- HTTP, TCP, and exec liveness probes
- Readiness probes for traffic control
- Startup probes for slow-starting applications

**Time:** 30 minutes

**Curriculum module:** [CKAD Part 3 - Application Probes](https://kube-dojo.github.io/k8s/ckad/part3-observability/module-3.1-probes/)
