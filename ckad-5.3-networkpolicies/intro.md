# NetworkPolicies

In this lab you will create NetworkPolicies to control traffic between pods. NetworkPolicies are a critical CKAD exam topic.

**What you will learn:**
- Default-deny policies
- Allow specific ingress traffic by label and namespace
- Control egress traffic
- Combined ingress/egress policies

**Time:** 40 minutes

**Curriculum module:** [CKAD Part 5 - NetworkPolicies](https://kube-dojo.github.io/k8s/ckad/part5-networking/module-5.3-networkpolicies/)
