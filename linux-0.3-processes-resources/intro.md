## Processes and Resources

Every running program on Linux is a process. Understanding how to inspect, control, and monitor processes is essential for troubleshooting and performance tuning.

### What You'll Learn

- List and inspect processes with `ps`, `pstree`, and `top`
- Control processes with signals: `kill`, `bg`, `fg`, `nohup`
- Monitor resources with `free`, `df`, `du`, `vmstat`
- Understand process limits with `ulimit`
