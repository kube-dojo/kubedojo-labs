# Secrets Management

Kubernetes Secrets are base64-encoded by default, not encrypted. This lab covers securing secrets with encryption at rest, RBAC, and best practices.

## What You Will Learn

- Create and mount secrets securely
- Configure encryption at rest using EncryptionConfiguration
- Restrict secret access with least-privilege RBAC
