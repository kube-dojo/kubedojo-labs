# Congratulations!

You have completed the **Secrets Management** lab.

## Key Takeaways

- Secrets are base64-encoded by default, NOT encrypted
- Enable encryption at rest with EncryptionConfiguration
- Restrict secret access with least-privilege RBAC (get without list)
- Consider external secret management for production environments
